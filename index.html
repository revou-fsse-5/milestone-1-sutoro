About Us

Jacaranda Garden Maintenance has been providing exceptional gardening services to the Sydney's Lower North Shore for over 20 years. We are a dedicated team of passionate and experienced professionals who take pride in creating beautiful and healthy outdoor spaces for our clients.

Our Service

  • Gardening: We offer a comprehensive range of gardening services, including planting, weeding, mulching, garden bed maintenance, and more.
  • Mowing: Keep your lawn looking neat and tidy with our efficient mowing services. We can handle lawns of all sizes and complexities.
  • Hedging: We'll keep your hedges looking sharp and under control with our precise hedging skills.
  • General Strata Cleaning: We provide comprehensive cleaning services for strata properties, including garden maintenance, rubbish removal, and high-pressure cleaning.
